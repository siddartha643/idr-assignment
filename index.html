About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

IDR combines academic insight through university partnerships with real-world industry projects to train professionals capable of managing modern digital and AI risk challenges.

Our Model

Academy

Training bootcamps and certification programs for students and professionals in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new frameworks, AI governance models and digital risk research to support future organisations.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement risk and security frameworks such as ISO 27001, NIST and NIS2.

Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.

Our community includes professionals from financial services, technology companies and critical infrastructure organisations.
